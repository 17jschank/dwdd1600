Ability Scores

In D&D, ability scores help your character interact with the world. They are located on the left side of your character sheet. When you want to do something and your DM asks you to roll a die, you will roll a d20 and add the associated ability score.

One kind of roll you make is a skill check. When you're not fighting someone or reacting to something in the moment, you'll make a skill check. They are determined by your ability scores (and sometimes also your proficiency score). Your DM will let you know which skill to use.
